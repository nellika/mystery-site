BASIC RULES:

All of the competitors are individual players. You can match in teams during the race but you are not gaining points as a team. In every small competition or quiz in the cities on the way you participate individually. During moving between the cities you will be always tracked by you mentor. You have to visit attractions which you can find in our app and check in. Of course it is always up to you which you will choose. On your way you will probably meet a lot of new people. For sharing a photo with them in our app you can gain some extra points. (2* for one person). When you arrive to the destinations then you have to participate in small contest created by us. It is also opportunity to get better position in our ranking.
