bonvoyage

It is time to get game out of computer! You have an unique opportunity to participate in our race and explore Poland. Alone or in pairs you will hitchhike between Polish cities. On your way you have total freedom of choice. In our app you can find attractions for visiting which you can gain some extra points.

When you reach one of the destinations, we have prepared contests to get higher position in game ranking. On every stage of the competition you can change your partner or decide to go further alone. Remember, it is always up to you :)

App

Every Competitor will gain access to our APP. It contains maps and attractions. By using it you can check in when you complete some task, check out the ranking and share your HH memories with others players.

Gaining Points

5* - for taking new car
10* - for visiting attractions
2* - for every new friend
5* - for winning one of the competitions, quiz
-5* for taking the public means of transport

Accomodation

During this trip you will be hosted by the local families and also provided with rooms in the hostels on the way.

Guide

Your Guide is an experienced traveller for whom Poland is home. He is your advisor, your friend in need. In each city he will take care of your entertainment during competitions and answer you every question about sightseeing. He will be accompanying you through your journey. In most extreme cases he can provide a car

Welcome Package

In our welcome package we offer one meeting at which you can meet some hitchers, who will tell you about their experience and also give you some advices.
All you need is already packed. Except for your laundry. We have this toolbox of HH for you” Extern charger, raincoat, paper and pen, gifts for drivers as a nice gesture.
X Every competitor will be provided with small packages for the drivers. Some local mini drinks, postcards, mini flags. It is always nice to give something in exchange for a ride and it doesn’t necessarily need to be money :D
